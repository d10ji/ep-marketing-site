About Easy Prayogshala

Built for Nepal's
Diagnostic Labs

Easy Prayogshala (EP) is a comprehensive web-based Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) designed for pathology labs and diagnostic centers across Nepal.

It manages the complete laboratory workflow — from patient registration and billing, through sample collection and lab testing, to report preparation and dispatch. Supporting multi-branch operations, role-based access control, and full bilingual usage.

✓ Patient Registration ✓ Invoice & Billing ✓ Sample Tracking ✓ Report Generation ✓ Real-Time Dashboard ✓ eSewa / Khalti / PhonePay
Why Choose EP

Everything Your Lab Needs

One platform to manage every aspect of your pathology lab — faster, smarter, built for Nepal.

Patient Management

Register patients once, find them instantly by name or mobile. Full history, emergency contacts, and address records.

Invoice & Billing

Generate professional invoices instantly. Accept Cash, eSewa, Khalti, and PhonePay. Track outstanding dues effortlessly.

Lab Workflow

Seamless 5-step pipeline: Patient → Invoice → Sample → Report → Dispatch. Each step time-stamped with full audit trail.

Real-Time Dashboard

Live KPIs, revenue trend charts, workflow funnel, and top investigations. Filter by Today, Week, Month, or Nepali Fiscal Year.

Multi-Branch Support

Run multiple lab locations from one account. Each branch has isolated data with head-office visibility for management.

Role-Based Access

Admin, Lab Technician, Receptionist, Accountant, Sample Collector — each role sees only their required modules and actions.
